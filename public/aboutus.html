Key Features of Training and Placement Software

1 . Placements Registration

Candidates can register for company placements online without requiring the assistance of a placement faculty member.The registration portal lets the student fill in the relevant information .

2 . Strong Outreach

With this software, you can speed up the communication process between the recruiting companies and the potential candidates, which ensures more placements.

3 . E-Notice Board

During the busy recruitment season, it can be frustrating to keep checking the mailbox every time to check the latest updates, as there is a high chance of missing critical notifications or recruitment deadlines.
